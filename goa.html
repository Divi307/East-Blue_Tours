Goa

Sun, Sand, and Serenity: Goa Travel Package

Welcome to Goa, India's tropical paradise! Known for its pristine beaches, vibrant nightlife, and Portuguese heritage, Goa is the perfect destination for relaxation, adventure, and cultural exploration. Whether you're a beach bum, a history buff, or a party enthusiast, Goa has something for everyone.


Package Highlights

Duration: 5 Days / 4 Nights
Best Time to Visit: November to February (peak season with pleasant weather).
Ideal For: Families, Couples, Solo Travelers, Groups


Itinerary Overview


Day 1: Arrival & North Goa Beaches

Welcome to Goa: Arrive at Goa International Airport or Madgaon Railway Station. Transfer to your hotel.
Calangute Beach: Relax at one of Goa's most popular beaches.
Baga Beach: Enjoy water sports like parasailing, jet skiing, and banana boat rides.
Evening: Explore the vibrant nightlife at Tito's Lane or Club Cubana.
Overnight Stay: 3-star or beach resort in North Goa.


Day 2: Explore North Goa

Fort Aguada: Visit this 17th-century Portuguese fort offering stunning views of the Arabian Sea.
Anjuna Beach: Known for its laid-back vibe and iconic flea market (on Wednesdays).
Chapora Fort: Relive the Bollywood magic of Dil Chahta Hai at this scenic fort.
Evening: Enjoy a beachside dinner at a shack.
Overnight Stay: 3-star or beach resort in North Goa.


Day 3: South Goa Beaches & Relaxation

Palolem Beach: Relax at this serene crescent-shaped beach.
Colva Beach: Enjoy the calm waters and white sands.
Dudhsagar Falls: Take a day trip to this majestic waterfall (optional add-on).
Evening: Enjoy a sunset cruise on the Mandovi River with live music and dance.
Overnight Stay: 3-star or beach resort in South Goa.


Day 4: Cultural Exploration

Old Goa: Visit UNESCO World Heritage Sites like Basilica of Bom Jesus and Se Cathedral.
Fontainhas: Explore the colorful Latin Quarter with its Portuguese-style houses.
Spice Plantation Tour: Experience a guided tour of a spice farm with a traditional Goan lunch.
Evening: Shop for souvenirs at Mapusa Market or Anjuna Flea Market.
Overnight Stay: 3-star or beach resort in South Goa.


Day 5: Departure

Morning: Relax at your hotel or visit a nearby beach.
Departure: Transfer to Goa International Airport or Madgaon Railway Station for your onward journey.
