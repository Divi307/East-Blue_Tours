Jaipur

Explore the Pink City: Jaipur Travel Package

Discover the royal heritage, vibrant culture, and architectural marvels of Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan. From majestic forts to bustling bazaars, Jaipur offers a perfect blend of history, tradition, and modernity.


Package Highlights

Duration: 3 Days / 2 Nights
Best Time to Visit: October to March
Ideal For: Families, Couples, Solo Travelers, History Enthusiasts


Itinerary Overview


Day 1: Arrival & City Exploration

Welcome to Jaipur: Arrive at Jaipur International Airport or Railway Station. Transfer to your hotel.
Hawa Mahal: Visit the iconic "Palace of Winds," a stunning example of Rajput architecture.
City Palace: Explore the royal residence, showcasing a blend of Mughal and Rajasthani styles.
Jantar Mantar: Discover the UNESCO World Heritage Site, an astronomical observatory.
Evening: Stroll through the local markets for handicrafts, textiles, and jewelry.
Overnight Stay: Luxury/Heritage Hotel in Jaipur..



Day 2: Forts & Cultural Immersion

Amber Fort: Visit the majestic fort with its Sheesh Mahal (Mirror Palace) and elephant rides (optional).
Jaigarh Fort: Explore the "Fort of Victory" and enjoy panoramic views of the city.
Nahargarh Fort: Witness the sunset from this hilltop fort.
Evening: Enjoy a traditional Rajasthani folk dance and music performance with dinner.
Overnight Stay: Luxury/Heritage Hotel in Jaipur.



Day 3: Local Experiences & Departure

Albert Hall Museum: Visit the oldest museum in Rajasthan.
Panna Meena Ka Kund: Explore the historic stepwell.
Shopping: Visit Johari Bazaar and Bapu Bazaar for souvenirs, gemstones, and textiles.
Departure: Transfer to the airport or railway station for your onward journey.



Inclusions:

Accommodation in a 4-star or heritage hotel.
Daily breakfast and one traditional Rajasthani dinner.
Private AC vehicle for sightseeing and transfers.
Entrance fees to monuments and attractions.
Guided tours with a local expert.



Exclusions:

Airfare/Train tickets.
Personal expenses and tips.
Optional activities like elephant rides or camel safaris.
