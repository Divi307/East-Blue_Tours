About Us - East Blue Tours

Welcome to East Blue Tours, your go-to guide for exploring the amazing beauty, culture, and diversity of India. We love helping travelers like you discover the best of this incredible country, from its famous landmarks to its hidden treasures.

At East Blue Tours, we believe traveling is about more than just seeing new places—it’s about experiencing new cultures, trying delicious food, and making memories that will stay with you forever. Whether you want to visit the snowy Himalayas, the peaceful backwaters of Kerala, the sandy deserts of Rajasthan, or the lively streets of Mumbai, we’re here to make your trip easy, fun, and unforgettable.

What We Do

Our goal is to show you the best of India. We create travel plans, share useful tips, and offer personalized advice for all kinds of travelers—whether you’re traveling alone, with family, as a couple, or in a group.

Why Travel with Us?

Local Knowledge: Our team knows India well and can help you discover its true essence.
Customized Trips: We design trips that suit your interests, whether you want adventure, relaxation, culture, or luxury.
Responsible Travel: We care about the environment and local communities, so we promote travel that is respectful and sustainable.
Always Here for You: From planning your trip to being there during your journey, we offer support every step of the way.

Our Story

East Blue Tours was created by people who love India and love to travel. We know how overwhelming planning a trip can be, so we’re here to make it simple and stress-free. Our goal is to help you experience the real India—its beauty, its people, and its culture. Let us help you explore India in a way that’s meaningful and memorable. Join us on a journey to discover the heart of this incredible country.
