Lakshadweep

Discover the Untouched Paradise: Lakshadweep Travel Package

Escape to the pristine islands of Lakshadweep, a hidden gem in the Arabian Sea. Known for its turquoise lagoons, coral reefs, and white sandy beaches, Lakshadweep offers a serene and untouched tropical paradise. Perfect for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and those looking to unwind in a tranquil setting.


Package Highlights

Duration: 5 Days / 4 Nights.
Best Time to Visit: October to March (ideal weather for water activities).
Ideal For: Couples, Families, Adventure Enthusiasts, Nature Lovers


Itinerary Overview


Day 1: Arrival & Agatti Island

Welcome to Lakshadweep: Arrive at Agatti Airport.
Transfer to your resort by boat.
Agatti Island: Relax on the stunning beaches and enjoy the crystal-clear waters.
Evening: Enjoy a beachside barbecue dinner.
Overnight Stay: Beach resort on Agatti Island.


Day 2: Bangaram Island Exploration

Bangaram Island: Take a day trip to this uninhabited island, known for its coral reefs and marine life.
Snorkeling & Swimming: Explore the vibrant underwater world.
Evening: Return to Agatti and enjoy a traditional island dinner.
Overnight Stay: Beach resort on Agatti Island.


Day 3: Kavaratti Island

Kavaratti Island: Visit the capital of Lakshadweep, known for its beautiful lagoons and marine aquarium.
Water Sports: Enjoy activities like kayaking, paddleboarding, and glass-bottom boat rides.
Evening: Relax by the beach and enjoy a cultural performance by local artists.
Overnight Stay: Beach resort on Kavaratti Island.


Day 4: Kalpeni Island

Kalpeni Island: Explore this picturesque island with its coral debris and shallow lagoons.
Scuba Diving: Dive into the clear waters to witness the vibrant coral reefs and marine life (optional add-on).
Evening: Enjoy a sunset cruise around the island.
Overnight Stay: Beach resort on Kalpeni Island.


Day 5: Departure

Morning: Relax on the beach or take a final swim in the lagoon.
Departure: Transfer to Agatti Airport for your onward journey.


Inclusions

Accommodation in a beach resort.
Daily breakfast and one traditional island dinner.
Boat transfers between islands.
Guided tours with a local expert.


Exclusions

Airfare to and from Lakshadweep.
Personal expenses and tips.
Water sports and adventure activity charges.
Scuba diving and other optional add-ons.
